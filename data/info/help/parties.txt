= Recruiting =

Recruiting is scenario dependent.

= Controlling Party Members =

You can take full control of any party member at any time, however you can never control more than one character at a time. To take control of a character press the hotkey shown next to his name. You can use tactical commands to exercise indirect control over AI controlled party members.

= Dismissing Party Members =

You can dismiss a member of your party if you want to. Just trigger the character you want to dismiss to start a dialogue; making the character leave the party will be one of the available dialogue options.
